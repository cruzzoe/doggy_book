About

Dog Booker is a free dog booking system created by Oni's parents during lockdown to help dogs feel more confident around other human and dog friends. Anyone in St Paul's Way with dogs can create an account to book their neighbour's dog to look after for a few hours. Feel free to use the first one or two sessions together to get to know each other if you are new to your neighbour.

You can choose when you want your dogs to be avaialble from My Dog. Once your dog is booked, you will get an email notification from Dog Booker. If you are no longer available for the slots booked by your neighbours, please delete your dog's available slots from My Dog. Email confirming cancellations will be sent. Please make a suitable arrangement with your neighbour for the return of your dog.
