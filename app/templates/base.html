About

Dog Booker is a free schedulling system created by Oni's parents during lockdown to help dogs feeling more confident around other human and dog friends. Anyone in St Paul's Way with dogs can create an account to book their neighbour's dog to look after for a few hours.

Netiquette and etiquette refer to rules of good behaviour online and in person. We are all lovely people and make sure we are part of a safe and respectful community when interacting with each other.

About Dog Booker

Dog Booker is designed by Oni's parents for people who love dogs and free to use. Please register yourselves first and then register your beloved dogs for their fun day out with your lovely neighbours.

How to Use

You can choose when you want your dogs to be avaialble from a schedule section. Once your dog is booked, you will get a notification email from the site. Please make a suitable arrangement with your neighbour for the return of your dog.

Disclaimer

While every effort is made, the users assume all responsibility and risk of the use of this website. Dog Boooker is only open to St Paul's Way residents but please ensure you only allow your neighbours to take your dog.
