FAQs in 3 easy steps!

Step 1. Register your dog under the My Dog Section.

Step 2. Add in slots for your dog in the same section for when your dog is available.
These will be shown to others to book. The person who makes the booking is responsible for arranging the meetup & hosting the dog.

Step 3. To book some doggy time with another dog, select New Booking and pick a dog and a time that suits you. An email will be sent out to you and the dog parent to notify of the booking.
If you need to cancel/delete your slot, this can be done from
i) the My Bookings Page if you are borrowing a dog
ii) the My Dog page if your dog becomes available as a parent.
